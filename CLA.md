## How to Sign

You do not need to sign and return this document manually. When You open a pull request,
the CLA Assistant bot will check whether You have already agreed to this Agreement. If You
have not, the bot will comment on Your pull request with instructions. To agree, post a
pull-request comment containing exactly:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your GitHub username, the signed commit, and a timestamp are recorded in
`.github/cla-signatures/` in this repository as the record of agreement. One signature
covers all of Your present and future Contributions to the Project.

### Individual contributors

Sign as described above using Your personal GitHub account. By signing You agree to this
Agreement in Your individual capacity.

### Contributing on behalf of an employer or other entity (Corporate CLA)

If You are contributing as part of Your employment, or Your employer otherwise has rights
to intellectual property You create, an authorized representative of the entity must
agree to this Agreement on the entity's behalf. To arrange a Corporate CLA covering
designated employees, contact the Maintainer at **yab@albazianlaw.com** before
contributing. Individual sign-off via the bot is still required for each contributing
employee account.
